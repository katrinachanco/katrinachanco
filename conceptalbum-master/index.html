Marianas Trench

“You always have to commit to doing something you feel is just beyond your means,” -Josh Ramsay

Before

I knew a few songs on the album inside and out, but was unfamiliar with the less popular songs

During

Listening to the whole album shocks me. The continuity of the album from one song to the next makes it sound like one hour-long song

After

I am glad to have learned more about this album because now, I have a better understanding of the songs I loved, and found a few new favorites along the way

Ever After Album

Why did I choose this Concept Album?

1) I have always been in love with Marianas Trench's music, especially all of the songs on this album.
2) The other albums I liked were not concept albums

Ever After Album - Music Videos

All of the Music Videos Marianas Trench made for this album are compiled into the one video shown below. They are sequenced in a specific order; so when played, the videos tell the story that Marianas Trench created.

The central female actress (Darla Taylor) is the same throughout all the videos. This was done as a means of continuity and to portray the story more convincingly.


Haven't Had Enough (0:00-4:06)
Fallout (4:06-8:45)
Desperate Measures (8:45-13:02)
Stutter (13:02-17:04)
By Now (17:04-23:24)
Story Line - Concert (23:24-30:03) *This section is additional footage the band plays at their lives concerts for this album. Each section would be played before a different song, and in this video they are compiled altogether.
